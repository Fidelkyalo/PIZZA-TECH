Who We Are

Pizza Technologies is a Nairobi-based technology company on a mission to close the digital skills gap across East Africa. Founded on the belief that access to quality technology education can transform communities, we combine classroom learning with real-world application — producing developers, digital professionals, and innovators ready to lead.

Our Mission

To make technology accessible, practical, and impactful — from the classroom to the boardroom. We build capacity in individuals and organisations through our coding programmes, cybersecurity advisory services, and strategic technology partnerships.

Why Pizza Technologies?

The name reflects our philosophy: just as a great pizza brings together distinct, high-quality ingredients into something greater than the sum of its parts — we bring together technology, education, and community to create extraordinary outcomes. We are not just a tech company. We are builders, educators, and problem solvers committed to East Africa's digital future.
